FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Exterior window cleaning is $10 per window or $5 per pane. Interior cleaning is $5 per window. Screen cleaning is $5 per screen. The average home runs about $350. Use our instant quote tool to get your exact price in 60 seconds.

Most Lakeway & Bee Cave jobs are scheduled within 7–14 days. Emergency appointments are sometimes available in as little as 48 hours. Call (512) 518-0512 for urgent scheduling.

For exterior-only cleaning, you don't need to be home. We just need access to your yard. For interior cleaning, someone needs to be present. We'll confirm all details before your appointment.

If rain affects your results within 7 days of your service, we come back and re-clean for free. No questions asked, no forms to fill out. Just call or text us.

Yes, CleanzATX carries $1,000,000 general liability insurance. Your home and property are fully protected on every job. We're happy to provide a certificate of insurance upon request.

We use a professional water-fed extension pole system that reaches up to 3 stories from the ground. Water is de-ionized (purified) so it leaves zero mineral deposits or streaks when it dries. Better results than traditional methods.

Yes, gutter cleaning can be added to any window cleaning or pressure washing visit. No extra trip needed. We clear debris and flush gutters clean, all in one appointment.
